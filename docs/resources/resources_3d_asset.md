## Poly Haven
<div class="custom-tags">
    <div class="custom-tag mesh">Models</div>
    <div class="custom-tag material">Materials</div>
    <div class="custom-tag hdri">HDRIs</div>
</div>
All assets on Poly Haven are free, available to use under the CC0 license, and are of very high quality! The library isn't the biggest (especially when it comes to models), but it's definitely not small.  
None of the assets are made with AI.  
[Main Page](https://polyhaven.com/)

## Superhive
<div class="custom-tags">
    <div class="custom-tag unique">Unique</div>
</div>
Superhive, previously Blender Market, is the primary marketplace for Blender products. It mostly has addons and asset-packs, but you can really find anything.  
Things here *could* be made with AI, there is no guarantee of the contrary.  
[Main Page](https://superhivemarket.com/)
